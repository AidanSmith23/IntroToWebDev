Come in

Come in and wait to be seated. One of our friendly staff will great you by the door and walk you to your table as soon as it is free.

Don't be scared by your table's setup, it will not be like your normal restaurant.

2

Order

Your waiter will talk you through the menu and will give any suggestions if you wish.

You can see our menu here.

Once you are ready, a waiter will come and take your order.

3

Cook your own

While you wait for your main, a selection of complementary starters will be brought to your table.

Also, our waiters will transform your table into your bbq.

When your food is ready, it will be brought to you to cook, but don't worry, our staff will also be moving from table to table to help.

4

Enjoy

Now, just sit back and enjoy your food however you wish.
